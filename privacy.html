Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

voii is a serverless peer-to-peer chat application. It is designed with privacy as a core principle. voii does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server operated by us — because no such server exists.

Data We Collect

None. voii has no backend server, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers

How Messages Work

Messages are sent directly from one device to another using Apple Push Notification service (APNs). The message flow is:

  • Sender device → Apple APNs servers → Recipient device

Messages pass through Apple's APNs infrastructure for delivery. We have no access to message content in transit. Once delivered, messages are stored only on the sender's and recipient's devices.

Data Stored on Your Device

voii stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Contacts: Device tokens and aliases of people you've added
  • Messages: Text content of messages you've sent and received
  • Groups: Group membership information

This data is stored in your device's app sandbox and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced across your own Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service. We have no access to your iCloud data.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, voii uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your contacts and messages across your own devices. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not operate or have access to the CloudKit containers — Apple manages them entirely.

APNs Authentication Key

voii embeds an Apple Push Notification service signing key within the app to send push notifications directly without a backend server. This key is used solely for authenticating push notification requests to Apple's servers.

Third-Party Services

voii uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs): For message delivery
  • CloudKit (iCloud): For syncing data across your devices

No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks are used.

Children's Privacy

voii does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we collect no data from anyone, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since voii is open source, all changes are visible in the public repository.
